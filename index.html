About Amy

One Crafty Momma 💕

Hi, I'm Amy Harrington!

Welcome to ScrapNSewMom — your go-to destination for handcrafted wreaths and home decor made with love right here in Winslow, Maine. I'm a crafty momma who turns fabric scraps, ribbon, and creativity into pieces that make your front door and home feel warm and inviting.

Whether you're looking for a seasonal wreath, a thoughtful handmade gift, or something uniquely beautiful for your home, I pour heart into every single piece I make. No two are exactly alike — just like the homes they decorate.

From Valentine's Day to the holidays and everything in between, there's always something new blooming in the workshop!
